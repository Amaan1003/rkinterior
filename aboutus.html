About Our Studio

Where architectural precision meets interior artistry to create spaces that inspire and endure.

Established with a vision to redefine luxury living, RK Interiors has been crafting extraordinary spaces since 2010. Our journey began in Mumbai with a single project and has blossomed into creating some of India's most exquisite interiors.

We believe interiors should tell a story - your story. Each space we design is a carefully curated composition of light, texture, and form, blending contemporary aesthetics with timeless elegance.

Our team of award-winning designers and craftsmen bring together decades of experience in both architecture and interior design, ensuring every project benefits from our holistic approach to space creation.

Our Design Philosophy

Architectural Integrity

We approach each project with architectural rigor, ensuring structural honesty while creating spaces that inspire. Our designs respect the building's form while enhancing its function.

Material Poetry

Materials speak volumes in our designs. We carefully select and combine textures to create tactile experiences that engage all senses and stand the test of time.

Light as Medium

Light transforms spaces. We design with natural and artificial light to create dynamic environments that change beautifully throughout the day and seasons.

Client-Centric

Your vision is our blueprint. We listen deeply to understand your lifestyle and aspirations, translating them into spaces that reflect your unique personality and needs.
